Mouse Accuracy

Mouse accuracy is simply how precise your clicks are when playing the game. Higher accuracy means you’ll reliably land abilities, move efficiently, and avoid misclicks that can cost you fights.

Warm-Up Exercise

A warm-up exercise I like is picking a spot on the minimap, such as a specific tower, and seeing how quickly and accurately I can click that point. Then I immediately center the camera back on my champion and repeat this a few times at the start of the game. This improves both speed and precision, making your in-game actions more consistent.
