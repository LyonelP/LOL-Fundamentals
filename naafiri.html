Diana Mid Matchup – Naafiri
← Back to Matchup Hub
51%

Matchup Notes

  • Naafiri has very weak early pushing. Diana can take full wave control from levels 1–3 with Q last-hits and passive AoE.
  • Her Q poke is annoying but predictable—sidestep the return path and she loses most of her trading power.
  • When Naafiri uses W aggressively, you can E her instantly to deny most of the burst. Her entire early trade pattern is front-loaded.
  • At level 6 Naafiri all-in is strong, but Diana wins if she saves W to absorb the initial burst then re-engages with R.
  • Naafiri struggles to farm under tower. Slow-pushing 2 waves and stacking a third can force her into losing HP or missing CS.
  • In sidelane Diana always wins extended fights—Naafiri’s kit heavily relies on one rotation, while Diana’s sustained DPS outscales her.
