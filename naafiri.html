Diana Mid Matchup – Naafiri
← Back to Matchup Hub
51%

Matchup Notes

  • Naafiri's dodgs die to one auto attack from a melee champion. This also stacks Diana's passive and Grasp so look to trade after killing one or two off.
  • Naafiri only has 4 seconds to cast her second Q. If you're hit by the first Q try to kill one dog immediatly with an auto and then play back to dodge the second one.
  • Naafiri can actually dodge Diana R with her E as well as her W. It's important to wait for both to be down or at least the W, if you can land the Q first to follow her E with your E.
