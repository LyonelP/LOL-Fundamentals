Understanding the Early Game

Level 1 is crucial because it sets the stage for the entire match. Goals at this stage include:

  • Have a successful invade.
  • Know where the enemy jungler is starting.
  • Know if the enemy is invading or late invading.

During the early game, timing is everything. Key objectives include:

  • Scuttle Crab Timing: The first scuttle spawns at 3:30. Knowing when and where it spawns helps track the enemy jungler's path and likely priorities.
  • Grub/Gromp/Small Jungle Camps: These camps spawn at 5:00. Efficient clears help you reach level 3–4 and prepare for lane pressure or early ganks.
  • First Dragon Timing: The first dragon spawns at 5:00. Early awareness allows your team to contest or secure it safely.

The early game phase generally ends around the 14-minute mark.
