Matchup Notes

  • Malzahar’s passive shield blocks your Q’s damage, but your Moonlight proc still applies.
  • Since his E deals damage in ticks, you can time the second part of your W to absorb it effectively.
  • Use Diana’s AoE abilities to quickly clear his Voidlings and maintain lane control.
  • Combining Stormsurge with Ignite is often enough to finish Malzahar during his ult after a full combo.
  • Be careful: Malzahar’s R can interrupt your dash mid-E.
