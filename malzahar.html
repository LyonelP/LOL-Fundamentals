Matchup Notes

  • Malzahar’s passive shield prevents the damage from your Q but the moonlight will still be applied.
  • Since his E is a tick you have time to proc the second part of your W to absorb the damage.
  • Use Diana’s AOE to clear out his voidlings.
  • Malzahar R can interrupt your dash mid E.
