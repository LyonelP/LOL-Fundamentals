Red vs Blue Side

Mirrored, Not Equal

The map is mirrored but not identical. This creates inherent advantages depending on which side you’re on. On blue side, top lane is naturally strong side and bot lane weak side, while the opposite tends to be true on red side.

Map Geometry & Vision

The walls and paths differ significantly. Bot side walls are much narrower starting from the mid lane side bush, which is also far easier to defend and play from than the top side equivalent.

This leads to pink wards being heavily favored towards bot lane. The banana bush is another powerful red side control point, especially with new Faelight interactions. Retreat paths to river are also much safer on bot side due to shorter distances to bushes and towers.

Safety & Gank Pressure

A crucial jump plant above Krugs allows mid laners to safely move behind the bot tower. There is no position bot lane can hold on red side that keeps them fully safe from coordinated ganks and dives.

Jungle Pathing & Strong Side

Jungle pathing determines strong side. On red side, pathing towards bot is optimal due to safety and the power of starting raptors or red buff.

Starting blue requires an extremely fast clear to finish five camps before the enemy can invade red bush. Fast clear junglers can avoid weak side river entirely, reset, and reach strong side scuttle with items before the enemy can contest both crabs.
