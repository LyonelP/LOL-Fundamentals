Reading Players

This is one of the most advanced skills in League of Legends, and takes a lot of games, experience, and game understanding to master.

Here are some patterns to look for in your opponent:

  • Aggression level: Do they try to fight as soon as they come to lane?
  • Pushing level: How quickly and often do they push the wave?
  • Roaming level: How often do they leave lane, and how often do they commit to a full roam?
  • Dodging patterns: Pay attention to which side they like to dodge. Many players won’t ever try dodging forward, even though it can be one of the most rewarding ways to dodge.
