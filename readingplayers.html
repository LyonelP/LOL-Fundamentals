Reading Players

Reading players is all about predicting what your opponents and teammates are likely to do based on their behavior, champion, and position. Observing patterns and tendencies allows you to make smarter decisions and stay one step ahead.

For example, if an enemy jungler consistently ganks your lane after pushing, you can anticipate it and play more defensively. Watching how teammates respond to your calls can also help you understand their playstyle and coordinate better.

Practicing this skill involves reviewing games, noticing how players react in different situations, and trying to predict their next moves in real time. The better you read players, the more effective your positioning, trades, and rotations will be.
