Laning Fundamentals

The laning phase in League of Legends can be broken down into a few core components:

  • Farming: Securing more last-hits per wave than your opponent.
  • Pushing: Pushing first so your opponent has a hard time trading, resetting, or roaming.
  • Pulling / Lane Positioning: Maintaining a safer lane position that doesn’t push too fast while overextending your opponent.
  • Positioning: Staying in the optimal spot to trade, avoid damage/ganks, and to pressure the wave.
  • Resource & Ability Management: Managing health, mana/energy, cooldowns, abilities, autos and summoner spells to maintain lane control.
