Laning Fundamentals

The goal of League is to destroy the enemy Nexus, and laning is about destroying the tier 1 tower. You force your opponent out of lane by a reset or a kill, allowing you and your minions to reach the enemy tower. Gain gold, deny your opponent, and pressure the tower effectively.

General Rules of Laning

  • The stronger you are in lane, the less you want to roam.
  • As a melee champion, wait for the wave to settle before stepping up.
  • Build resists based on both your lane opponent and the enemy jungler.
  • Tower plate or tempo? Stay long enough to justify taking a plate.
  • Recalls: Keep your opponent in lane by pulling/pushing the wave.

Mental Stack

  • Enemy Position
  • Wave State
  • Minions
  • Enemy & Teammate Position
  • Cooldowns
  • Resources
  • Vision
  • Runes
  • Items
  • Gold

If unsure, start at the top of the stack and work down.
