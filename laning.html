Laning Fundamentals

In LoL you always want to focus on what you can control. The three most important actions are your movement clicks on the map, your auto attacks, and your abilities. These inputs make up the majority of your laning and overall skill level. The difference between an Iron player and a Challenger player comes down to the timing and placement of these clicks.

Lane Levels

Level 1: Your initial movement is the most important as you’re limited to ~1 ability. It’s crucial you move with the wave and specifically the ranged creeps. Then this is when you’ll look to make your first action with an auto attack or ability. Abilities should always be prioritized and only be used on minions as a last resort. An exception is when you can use an ability to last hit like Annie Q and trade with Auto Attacks. Abilities can be used to bait out enemy spells as well if they expect you to Auto Attack to last hit. This will at least trade abilities evenly.
