Laning

The laning phase in League of Legends can be broken down into several core components that every player should master:

  • Farming: Securing minion kills efficiently to build gold and XP.
  • Pushing: Applying pressure by moving the wave towards your opponent’s tower.
  • Pulling: Bringing the wave closer to your tower safely to create favourable conditions.
  • Positioning: Staying in the optimal spot to trade, avoid ganks, and pressure the wave.
  • Abilities: Using your champion’s kit effectively in trades, wave control, and harass.
  • Resource and Ability Management: Managing your health, mana/energy, cooldowns and summoner spells to maintain lane control.
