Laning Fundamentals

In League of Legends, you should focus on what you can control. Your movement clicks, auto attacks, and abilities make up the majority of your laning phase and overall skill expression.

The difference between an Iron player and a Challenger player is not mechanics, but the timing and placement of these actions.

Goal of Laning:
Farm every minion possible while denying your opponent as many as possible, all while maximizing damage to your opponent and their tower.

Mental Stack

  • Enemy Position – Can they trade, all-in, or zone you right now?
  • Wave State – Is the wave pushing, pulling, frozen, or about to crash?
  • Enemy & Teammate Position – Jungler paths, roams, and support movement.
  • Cooldowns – What abilities are down on both sides?
  • Resources – Health, mana, potions, and sustain.
  • Runes – Proc windows like Electrocute, Conqueror, Bone Plating.
  • Items – Item spikes and component advantages.
  • Gold – Who can recall and return stronger first?

If you are unsure what to do, start at the top of the stack and work down.

Levels 1–6

Level 1

Your initial movement is critical. Move with the wave and position near the ranged minions. Abilities should be prioritized and only used on minions as a last resort unless your champion can last-hit safely with them.

Level 2

Level 2 creates the first real power spike. Track experience closely. If you hit level 2 first, you gain priority. If not, respect the enemy’s window.

Level 3

Most champions now have full basic kits. This is when ganks become more dangerous and wave positioning matters significantly more.

Level 4

Focus on wave control and recall timings. Small mistakes here compound quickly. Avoid unnecessary trades that disrupt good wave states.

Level 5

Pressure increases as players prepare for level 6. Look to stack waves or thin them depending on jungle position and matchup.

Level 6

Ultimate abilities change lane dynamics completely. Respect enemy kill pressure, and use your own ultimate to force recalls, secure kills, or damage the tower.
