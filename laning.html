Laning Fundamentals

The laning phase in League of Legends can be broken down into core components every player should master:

  • Farming: Securing more last-hits per wave than your opponent.
  • Pushing: Pushing first so your opponent has a hard time trading, resetting, or roaming.
  • Pulling / Lane Positioning: Maintaining a safer lane position that doesn’t push too fast while overextending your opponent.
  • Positioning: Staying in the optimal spot to trade, avoid ganks, and pressure the wave.
  • Abilities: Using your champion’s kit effectively in trades, wave control, and harass.
  • Resource & Ability Management: Managing health, mana/energy, cooldowns, and summoner spells to maintain lane control.
