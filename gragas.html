Diana Mid Matchup – Gragas
← Back to Matchup Hub
52%

Matchup Notes

  • Play back until you know which ability Gragas started. His best trade is going straight for an E at the start of the lane. If he starts W, you can wait for the damage reduction to be down and stack Manaflow Band easily.
  • When looking for all-ins, position immediately behind him so he can’t E away which is where your E naturally places you.
  • With his passive he'll always be able to freeze or pull the wave and look to ult you under his tower or wait for the jungler to come mid.
