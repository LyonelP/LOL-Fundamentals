K'SANTE MID MATCHUP
Back to Matchup Hub
48%

Matchup Notes

  • Mainly look for Q poke and avoid letting him proc Grasp on you.
  • Time your E trades when his Grasp and Q3 are down.
  • His Q3 can interrupt your E, and his W can absorb a lot of your burst. It can often be baited by moving up slightly after landing a Q.
  • Usually, it's not worth extended trades; by the time you're in lethal range, he'll often have backup.
