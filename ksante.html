Diana Mid Matchup – K'Sante
Back to Matchup Hub
48%

Matchup Notes

  • Mainly look for Q poke in this matchup and avoid letting him proc Grasp on you.
  • Time your E trades on him when his Grasp and Q3 are down.
  • His Q3 can interrupt your E and his W can absorb a lot of your burst, but it can be baited out by walking up slightly after landing a Q.
  • Most of the time it's not worth trading with him, since by the time you’re in lethal range, he'll have backup.
