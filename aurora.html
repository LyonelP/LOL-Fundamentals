Matchup Notes

  • Aurora's Q has a long animation and is hard to hit, if you're standing around the max range. Use this to bait it out and then safely walk up to thin out the wave and throw Q's.
  • You don’t have to wait for Aurora’s W to be down to look for an Ult. She’ll still be slowed from the ult and her W has a slight animation making it easy to tell where she is.
  • Diana R can be used to interrupt Aurora E. It can also be used to make it hard for her to make it to her ult wall by slowing and pulling her towards the center.
  • Be careful walking into Aurora, if she still has both her E and Q up.
