Matchup Notes

  • Mel has consistent poke and lane pressure; watch for spell combos.
  • Look for windows to engage after her abilities are used.
  • Wave management can bait her into unsafe trades.
  • Post-level 6, Mel’s ultimate is dangerous; engage when it’s on cooldown.
  • Use mobility to dodge key abilities and capitalize on cooldowns.
