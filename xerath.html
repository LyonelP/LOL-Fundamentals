Matchup Notes

  • Luckily they changed his E so it doesn't stun for 2 seconds at max range anymore so you can often still E ult even if you're hit by the stun if he wasn't paying attention.
  • Normally you'll have to look to E to the ranged creeps to engage with ult.
  • Level 1 he might come from fog of war with a fully charged Q so its best to wait to come to lane for a second.
