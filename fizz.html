Diana Mid Matchup – Fizz

53%

Matchup Notes

  • Fizz wins the level 1 trade if he can E your first Q. The Q has to be thrown at max range to avoid this. He can also win the matchup if he gets the push and hits level 2 first.
  • Fizz has to rely on jungle ganks to turn the matchup. Be careful when pushing for plates since his Q + R on the wave will win him the trade if he can chase you down the entire lane.
  • Watch out for Fizz’s Q onto a minion into R combo, especially when he's coming off a reset.
  • Once you have Hourglass and Banshee’s Veil, there’s very little Fizz can do against you.
