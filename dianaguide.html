Intro

Diana is a flexible AP carry with strong laning, great waveclear, and powerful mid-game skirmishing. She can teamfight, splitpush, take objectives quickly, and snowball hard with tempo.

Runes

  • Electrocute
  • Sudden Impact
  • Eyeball Collection
  • Relentless Hunter

Build

  • Rocketbelt / Night Harvester
  • Nashor’s Tooth
  • Zhonya’s Hourglass

Combos

  • Q → E → W → Auto
  • E → R → W → Q

Laning

Use Q to control the wave and punish last hits. Hold E until you can force a winning trade.

Teamfighting

Look for flanks. Diana excels when pulling multiple targets with R and bursting priority carries.

Extra Tips

  • Passive works on inhibitors and nexus at specific angles
  • W shield timing wins trades
  • Q through minions hides its arc
