She can make picks, teamfight well, splitpush, push waves quickly, farm jungle camps, and has great damage for dragons and barons. Laning

Focus on farming while poking with Q. Use W to shield trades and E for aggressive all-ins. Look for jungle gank setups.

Warding

Keep river and jungle entrances warded. Prioritize vision around mid lane and dragon.

Farming / Pushing

Clear waves with Q + W efficiently. Farm jungle camps when possible to maintain level advantage.

Teamfighting

Look for flanks and peel priority targets. Use R to pull enemies into your team. Use W defensively if needed.

Splitpushing

Push sidelanes with Q + W. Teleport in for fights when your team needs help.

Closing Out The Game

Focus on objectives, baron, and inhibitors. Use vision control and force fights around your advantages.

Threats & Synergies

Diana struggles against ranged poke champions early. Synergizes well with engage supports and peel-heavy junglers.

Pros & Cons

  • Pros: Strong burst, flexible mid-game, good objective damage.
  • Cons: Weak vs ranged poke early, can be kited.

Extra Tips

  • Use passive reset on jungle camps to clear faster.
  • W can be used to absorb turret shots while all-ining.
  • Always try to hit R through minions for AoE pulls.
