Intro

Diana is a high-tempo AP melee carry who thrives on wave control, objective pressure, and explosive mid-game fights. She excels at punishing immobile champions, creating numbers advantages, and converting small leads into decisive map control.

Runes

  • Conqueror for extended fights and scaling damage
  • Electrocute when facing squishy, immobile lanes
  • Resolve secondary when trading into poke or burst
  • Attack speed shard is mandatory for passive uptime

Build

  • Nashor’s Tooth is core for DPS and waveclear
  • Rocketbelt enables gap closing and engage angles
  • Zhonya’s is non-negotiable against burst or CC
  • Void Staff scales extremely hard into mid-game fights

Summoner Spells

  • Flash is required for engage and escape
  • Ignite for kill pressure and snowball lanes
  • Teleport only when side-lane pressure is critical

Abilities

  • Max Q first for waveclear and poke
  • W second for shield uptime and trading
  • E last as it scales via resets, not ranks
  • Always plan passive procs before trading

Combos

  • Q → E → Auto → W for short trades
  • Q → E → R → Auto reset for all-ins
  • Flash R can catch backline carries off guard

Laning

  • Control waves early to avoid jungle pressure
  • Trade when passive is stacked
  • Crash waves before roaming or resetting

Warding

  • Ward raptors or pixel brush at level 3
  • Deep wards after first crash enable roams
  • Swap to sweeper once lane priority is secured

Farming / Pushing

  • Q + passive clears waves extremely fast
  • Take jungle camps when lanes are pushed
  • Always prep waves before objectives

Teamfighting

  • Flank whenever possible instead of front-to-back
  • Track enemy CC before committing
  • Zhonya’s timing decides fights

Splitpushing

  • Diana melts towers with passive
  • Pressure sides when teleport is available
  • Disengage early if vision is lost

Closing Out The Game

  • Force fights around objectives
  • Deny vision before engaging
  • Use Baron to split and collapse

Threats & Synergies

  • Hard CC mages are difficult matchups
  • Engage junglers amplify Diana’s strengths
  • Peel-heavy teams reduce assassination angles

Pros & Cons

  • Strong scaling and burst
  • Excellent objective damage
  • Vulnerable when cooldowns are down

Extra Tips

  • Passive works on inhibitors and nexus only at specific angles
  • Q minions before fights to preload passive
  • Use fog of war to mask engage paths
