Diana Mid Matchup – Jayce
← Back to Matchup Hub
52%

Matchup Notes

  • Jayce has strong poke and burst in early trades, be cautious pre-level 6.
  • Manage minion waves to avoid getting hit by Shock Blast combos.
  • All-ins are stronger post-level 6; watch for his Acceleration Gate + Hyper Charge.
  • Try to bait his abilities before committing to extended trades.
  • Positioning is key to dodge his ranged harassment while setting up your own trades.
