Computer / Peripherals

I did the majority of my initial climbing on a laptop with less than 60 FPS and a wireless mouse. Eventually, I upgraded to a desktop. Nowadays, most people have computers that can run League pretty well. I recommend playing at 144 FPS on a 144Hz monitor, but anything above 60 FPS is sufficient.

Mouse Settings

Having consistent and good mouse settings is important. I have my mouse at 1600 DPI and keep the in-game sensitivity at 50.

Ping / Internet

This is important to a degree. When I was climbing for Rank 1, my internet wouldn't work from 6 PM to midnight, so I would wake up at 1 AM every day and grind for 12–16 hours. Ping below 60 is ideal but 100 ping won’t stop you from hitting Challenger. Although, it becomes difficult above 150.
