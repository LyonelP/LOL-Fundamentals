Diana Mid Matchup – Kassadin
← Back to Matchup Hub
50%

Matchup Notes

  • Kassadin is weak early; pressure him pre-level 6 to gain lane control.
  • Watch for his Riftwalk combos post-level 6; avoid overextending.
  • Use minion wave manipulation to force him to use his mobility defensively.
  • All-ins are safer when his abilities are on cooldown.
  • Maintain vision and track jungle ganks to capitalize on his squishiness.
