Champion Select

The game starts in champion select. Have up to 5 bans ready in case your team happens to ban all of the other 4 most ban-worthy champions.

Check Side

Check which team is on which side. First pick is always blue side. This helps you anticipate lane matchups and draft accordingly.

Visualize the Game

As each pick is locked in, think about how that champion interacts with your champion and others. Visualize how the game will play out before it starts. View matchup guides for reference.
