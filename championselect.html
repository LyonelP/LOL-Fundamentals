Bans

Bronze

  • Bans champions they recently lost to.
  • Usually emotional or random bans.
  • Often ignores current meta picks.
  • May not know what champions are strongest.

Platinum

  • Bans strong meta champions more consistently.
  • Understands matchup bans.
  • Protects comfort picks.
  • Starts adapting bans based on role.

Challenger

  • Uses bans to shape the entire draft.
  • Protects team win conditions.
  • Removes champions that break compositions.
  • Adapts bans based on side selection and pick order.
  • Always has multiple bans prepared.

Side Selection

Bronze

  • Usually doesn’t notice side selection.
  • Blind picks without thinking about draft order.

Platinum

  • Knows blue side gets first pick.
  • Understands counterpick value.
  • Starts considering safer blind picks.

Challenger

  • Immediately identifies draft flow from side selection.
  • Blue side prioritizes overpowered first picks.
  • Red side prioritizes counterpick lanes.
  • Understands which roles need last pick most.

Team Composition

Bronze

  • Picks comfort regardless of team comp.
  • Doesn’t think about damage types.
  • Often drafts full AD or double AP accidentally.

Platinum

  • Understands basic AD/AP balance.
  • Starts thinking about engage and scaling.
  • Recognizes some bad draft combinations.

Challenger

  • Avoids double AP mid/jungle in most games.
  • Understands how Merc Treads and Null-Magic Mantle can take over the map.
  • Drafts around damage profile, engage, scaling, side lanes, and objective control.
  • Recognizes exceptions like champions with fixed build paths.

Visualizing the Game

Bronze

  • Focuses mostly on their own lane.
  • Doesn’t think about how champions interact together.

Platinum

  • Starts recognizing lane matchups.
  • Can predict some jungle pressure and win conditions.

Challenger

  • Visualizes the game before it starts.
  • Predicts lane states, jungle paths, and objective setups.
  • Knows which champions will control tempo and map movement.
  • Understands win conditions from champion select alone.
