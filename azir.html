Diana Mid Matchup – Azir
← Back to Matchup Hub
50%

Matchup Notes

  • This is another match up that's relatively easy to land Qs in since he has low base movement speed and doesn't move while attacking with his soldiers.
  • Look to trade when Azir uses Q with two soldiers out.
  • You can now E through his wall if you wait 1 second after the ult.
  • Azir will look to ult you under tower if you're past the side bush wall. You can also try to bait his ult this way or take the ult if you land Q first and then use the second E after 1 second to gap close.
