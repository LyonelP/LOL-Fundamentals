Matchup Notes

  • Always try to get the auto on LeBlanc if you’re going to be hit by her W.
  • Your R can be used to cancel her first W, but not the second, so only use on engage.
  • Don’t panic if you die to an all-in or gank. You still outscale and can 1v1 her at all stages.
