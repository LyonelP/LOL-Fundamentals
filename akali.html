Matchup Notes

  • Akali wins this matchup lvl 1 and 2. You can trade Q for Q lvl 1 but her second Q needs to be dodged.
  • Post reset is your best chance to win the matchup. Look for full Q E combos or E Auto trades with passive.
  • At 6 your ideal all in is interrupting her E with your ult but be careful of her R timing.
  • Look to trade with Akali immediately after she autos a minion.
  • Akali's biggest weakness is her waveclear. Fast push non-cannon waves if uncontested.
  • Be careful playing up in lane as Akali can chase you down long lanes.
