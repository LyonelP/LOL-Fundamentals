Diana Mid Matchup – Akali
← Back to Matchup Hub
48%

Matchup Notes

  • Akali wins the first two levels — avoid extended trades, trade Q for Q then dodge her second Q.
  • Your best window is post-reset. Look for Q → E trades or passive autos if she isn’t controlling the wave.
  • At 6, you win if you interrupt her E with your R — but be careful of her dodging your ult with her own.
  • Trade after she autos a minion so she can’t use her passive immediately.
  • Fast push non-cannon waves — she struggles to last-hit under tower.
  • Don’t walk too far up mid lane; Akali can chase with spacing while avoiding tower aggro.
