Matchup Notes

  • Akali wins this matchup lvl 1 and 2. You can trade Q for Q lvl 1 but her second Q needs to be dodged. This often leads to a reset level 3 unless the Akali didn't play aggressively early.
  • Post reset is your best chance to win the matchup. Look for full Q E combos or E Auto trades with passive, if she's not denying you from auto attacking the wave.
  • At 6 your ideal all in is interrupting her E with your ult but be careful of her casting R at the same time to dodge your ult.
  • Look to trade with akali immediately after she autos a minion so she cant use her passive immediately.
  • Akali's biggest weakness is her waveclear. Try to fast push all non canon waves if the Akali isn’t contesting your push.
  • Be careful playing up in lane as Akali can chase you down the lane and space her abilities well without taking tower damage.
