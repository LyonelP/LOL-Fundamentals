Diana Mid Matchup – Akali
← Back to Matchup Hub
48%

Matchup Notes

  • Akali wins this matchup lvl 1 and 2. You can trade q for q lvl 1 but her second q needs to be dodged. This often leads to a reset level 3 unless the Akali didn't play aggressively early.
  • Post reset is your best chance to win. Look for full Q→E combos or E auto trades with passive if she isn’t denying you from hitting the wave.
  • At 6 your ideal all-in is interrupting her E with your R, but be careful of her using R to dodge your ult.
  • Trade with Akali immediately after she autos a minion so she can't use her passive immediately.
  • Akali's biggest weakness is waveclear. Fast push all non-cannon waves if she’s not contesting. Look for easy Qs when she farms under tower.
  • Be careful playing up as Akali can chase you down the lane and space her abilities well without taking tower damage.
