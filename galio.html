Diana Mid Matchup – Galio
← Back to Matchup Hub
48%

Matchup Notes

  • Look for as many q's as possible lvl 1 before he levels up w and has the magic shield. He'll naturally push the wave back with his q and passive. Avoid his passive at all costs, especially level 1.
  • Position close but away from the ranged creeps since that's where he wants to throw his q.
  • Your ability to kill him depends a lot on whether or not they took aftershock.
  • If he goes for an e trade you almost always want to look for a long return trade. Just make sure the jungler isn't around. Since Galio naturally pushes you don't have to worry about him freezing the wave and can be patient waiting at tower for the wave instead of setting yourself up to be ganked.
