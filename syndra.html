← Back to Matchup Hub
51%

Matchup Notes

  • Baiting out her Qs and becoming familiar with her q cooldown is key for the first couple of levels.
  • Syndra’s best play at the start of a new wave is looking for a Q E immediately on you and throwing W afterwards. Wait out this combo before moving too far up.
  • Post 6 you always want to look for an ult trade after she goes for her Q E.
