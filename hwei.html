Diana Mid Matchup – Hwei
← Back to Matchup Hub
50%

Matchup Notes

  • Hwei has moderate poke; trade when her abilities are on cooldown.
  • Focus on dodging her skillshots to maintain lane control.
  • Post-level 6, she can be punished if her ultimate is used poorly.
  • Wave management is key; freeze or slow push to bait overextensions.
  • All-ins are viable with proper combo timing and positioning.
