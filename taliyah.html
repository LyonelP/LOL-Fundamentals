Matchup Notes

  • Her E makes it so you can't ult after you E.
  • You also have to be careful moving around the map against Taliyah. You want to stick to playing for your lane in this matchup.
  • Look for opportunities to go for a E W AA Q trade when she’s not expecting it, so you don’t get stunned by her E. Taliyahs will often then look for the E after the trade is already over and knock you away to safety with her W.
