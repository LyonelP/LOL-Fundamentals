Matchup Notes

  • Track his ferocity stacks — don’t fight when he has 4.
  • Ward bushes, not paths. Rengar only cares about angles.
  • Hold CC for after his leap, not before.
  • If he ults, group slightly to deny isolation picks.

Level 1 Ward

Rengar cheese paths revolve around top-side river and tri-bush angles. Early warding bushes is higher value than river vision.

Where to Start

Start opposite side of Rengar and path toward lanes with CC. Avoid early isolated 1v1s unless you have vision control.
