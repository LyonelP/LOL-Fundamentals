Sieging

Sieging is the process of pushing for tower damage with multiple team members grouped together. The main goal of a siege is chipping and breaking the tower while keeping your team safe from enemy engage or poke.

Core Concepts of Sieging

  • Wave Setup: Build a wave before crashing minions under tower reduces its resistances and lets your team come into range without taking turret aggro.
  • Vision Control: Ward for potential flanks and clear wards that the enemy can TP to.
  • Poke and Threat: Slowly chip the tower or enemy HP to make the next push lethal.
  • Positioning: Position further up the lower the enemy tower is.
  • Patience: Most failed sieges come from diving too early or overstaying once the wave is low or cleared.
