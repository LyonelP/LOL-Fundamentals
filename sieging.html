Sieging

Sieging is the process of pushing for tower damage with multiple team members grouped together. The main goal of a siege is chipping and breaking the tower while keeping your team safe from enemy engage or poke.

Core Concepts of Sieging

  • Wave Setup: Build a wave before grouping — crashing minions under tower reduces its resistances and lets your team come into range without taking turret aggro.
  • Vision Control: Clear flanks and river wards to make it unsafe for the enemy to engage.
  • Positioning: Keep poke champs and ADC in front range, bruisers and engage tools at the sides ready to punish oversteps.
  • Poke and Threat: Slowly chip the tower or enemy HP to make the next push lethal.
  • Patience: Most failed sieges come from diving too early or overstaying after a wave is cleared.
