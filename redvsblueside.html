Intro to Strong Side

Why is there a difference?

The map is mirrored but not identical. This creates inherent advantages depending on which side you’re on.

Map Geometry

The walls and paths differ significantly. Bot side walls are much narrower starting from the mid lane side bush, which is also far easier to defend and play from than the top side one.

This leads to pink wards being heavily favored towards bot lane. The banana bush is another powerful red side control point, especially with new Faelight interactions. Retreat paths are also much safer on bot side.

Walls and Pathing

A crucial jump plant above Krugs allows mid laners to safely move behind the bot tower. There is no position bot lane can hold on red side that keeps them fully safe from coordinated ganks and dives.

Jungle Pathing & Strong Side

Jungle pathing also determines strong side. On red side, pathing towards bot is optimal due to safety and the power of starting raptors or red buff.

Starting blue requires an extremely fast clear to finish five camps before the enemy can invade red bush. Fast clear junglers can avoid weak side river entirely, reset, and reach strong side scuttle with items before the enemy can contest both crabs.
