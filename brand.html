Diana Mid Matchup – Brand
← Back to Matchup Hub
52%

Matchup Notes

  • Bait out Brand’s W. His w has a delay which gives you room to dodge the ability. You can also use E to dodge it on him or even a minion if necessary especially if they have moonlight on them.
  • You can almost always use the combo of Qing the wave and waiting to E to the ranged creeps to go for the ultimate. Once brand is pulled in he has a significantly harder time landing the stun since he'll have to e or w first. If he goes for the w you can often use e to dodge it. If he goes for the e first then you can use e to also dodge the q or to soak some of the stun duration. Your q will also be back up after that point and you can go for a full combo.
