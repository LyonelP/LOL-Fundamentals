Diana Mid Matchup – Annie
← Back to Matchup Hub
50%

Matchup Notes

  • Watch out for Annie’s auto attack since she has 625 auto attack range. Luckily it’s easy to gain wave control in this matchup with Annie’s poor wave clear.
  • If you're going to take a stun from Annie’s Q then it can be best to go for an E trade with her to negate some of that stun duration.
  • Past level 5 you can always get the push in this matchup as long as you watch out for ganks.
  • Post 6 Annie can start using Tibers even if you're full health to gain control of the lane. This will push the wave but you can simply reset and wait to win the matchup in the sidelane where it's most favorable.
