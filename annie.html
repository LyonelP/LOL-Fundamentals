Diana Mid Matchup – Annie
← Back to Matchup Hub
50%

Matchup Notes

  • Annie has strong burst and stun potential; avoid getting caught by her passive stack.
  • Try to bait Tibbers before committing to trades.
  • Use your shield and Q combos to poke safely and manage wave control.
  • All-ins are easier after she uses her abilities; track cooldowns carefully.
  • Position to the sides to dodge her Q+W poke and prevent stuns in early levels.
