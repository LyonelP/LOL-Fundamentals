Summoner Spell Usage

This is a really underrated skill. The most basic example is using Flash to dodge a play completely instead of flashing later after having taken damage and Flash having to be used anyways.

Flash

Flash is the most used summoner spell in the game since the instant ability to move a substantial distance is extremely strong. The most basic example is using Flash to dodge a play completely instead of flashing later after having taken damage and Flash having to be used anyways. Knowing which walls can be flashed and from where is also extremely important, as well as which abilities can be extended or altered with Flash.

Ghost

How early this should be casted is the main distinguisher for skill on this summoner spell. It also increases one’s ability to kite because more distance can be spread between each auto attack.

Heal

Heal timing can be difficult as the healing can be reduced from healing reduction. You also want to maximize the burst of movement speed it gives. The trickiest part about the summoner spell is knowing when to sacrifice it for a teammate. If they still died when using it and it barely helped you, then you know it was a mistake to use it then.

Ignite

Knowing exactly how much damage it does for the execute and only using it if you didn’t have the time and damage them without. It should pretty much only be used early when playing support or when using it for the healing reduction.

Exhaust

Great against certain champions like Yasuo, Zed, Diana, and Talon that have strong ultimates with a short delay on them. It’s even better on champions that have a hard time exiting the fight or waiting out the Exhaust. It’s also mostly used bot lane on supports without hard crowd control.

Teleport

This is the most complicated summoner spell in the game, although it was simplified early by giving it such a long channel. Now it’s mostly used as a reset tool during lane. Ideally you make it back to lane before the enemy has started or finished their recall. If it’s past 10 minutes you can TP straight to the wave to start pushing it into the enemy tower faster, since the easiest way to keep someone in lane is to fast push the wave into tower.

Cleanse

Possibly taken mid against a champion like Lissandra, but even then Teleport is often still the better choice. Cleanse is mostly taken on ADCs against champions like Ashe. Timing a Cleanse perfectly after being CC’d is always impressive.

Smite

Everyone knows how important timing Smite is for securing objectives like Baron and Dragon. The only thing to track now is when your Smite deals 600, 900, or 1200 damage. Smite can also be used to clear out minions or, when fully upgraded, to clear a camp quickly.

Barrier

Barrier is mostly taken on champions that can't take advantage of the movement from Ghost or Heal. Barrier is good on champions like Xerath who are stationary while ulting. It’s now mostly taken on ADC champions after the last buff.
