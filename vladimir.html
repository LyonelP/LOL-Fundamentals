Matchup Notes

  • The scariest setup Vladimir can run is Aery with Ghost + Ignite. If he runs this, wait until level 3 to trade.
  • If he runs Phase Rush or Ignite, you can contest push and trade Qs level 1.
  • Be careful using E on Vladimir, especially on dives — his W slow is brutal.
  • His biggest weakness is poor waveclear early.
  • If you go OOM without killing him, reset — he usually can’t shove fast enough.
  • Ward and take enemy raptors to deny his sustain.
  • Using R early or post-reset is often best — he’s less likely to instantly W.
