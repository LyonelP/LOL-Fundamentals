Matchup Notes

  • The scariest setup Vladimir can run is Aery with Ghost Ignite. If he runs that setup you often have to wait until level 3 to trade. If he runs Phase Rush or Ignite, then you can look to gain the push and trade Q’s level 1.
  • Be careful using E on Vladimir, especially when tower diving since his W slows by 40%.
  • His biggest weakness is that he can't push quickly.
  • If you run out of mana before you can kill him, you can often reset and try again since he'll likely still be in lane.
  • Try to ward and take the enemy raptors against Vladimir so he can’t syphon off of them.
  • It's often best to use your ult very early in the trade or after a reset since he won't be as likely to W immediately.
