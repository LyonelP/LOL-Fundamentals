Matchup Notes

  • If he lands multiple Q's level 1 he can look to walk up to proc the passive for a winning trade.
  • Locke can dodge your Q with his E.
  • Diana can interrupt the second part of Locke's E with her ult.
  • E trades are often too quick for him to respond with W on time.
  • Look to trade back aggressively with autos if Locke jumps on you. It's only worth trying to dodge his Q to prevent stacks but at close range he'll be weaving autos in between Q's which you can out DPS.
