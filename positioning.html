Positioning

The best way to position is to be right outside the enemy’s danger range. This means understanding the effective reach of their key abilities and attacks, and standing just beyond them so you can react first when they commit.

In lane, you’re often standing parallel to your opponent to continue farming and last hitting while staying at the maximum distance that still allows you to contest minions. Proper positioning ensures safety while maximizing your ability to trade, punish mistakes, and capitalize on cooldown windows.

During teamfights, positioning is about finding safe zones behind your frontline while staying close enough to apply damage or utility. Always consider escape routes, terrain, and enemy threats. Good positioning minimizes risk and maximizes impact in every fight.
