Understanding the Meta

The meta in League changes every two weeks during the season, on Wednesday morning. Knowing what changes went live—especially right after a patch—can give you a huge advantage. For example, if a champion's base health was just nerfed, the enemy's advantage might shift.

The "meta" refers to the most effective strategies, champions, and playstyles at a given time in League of Legends. That's why playing as many games as possible within a few patches is best to become extremely familiar with the meta.
