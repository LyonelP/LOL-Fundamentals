Diana Mid Matchup – Karthus
← Back to Members
53%

Matchup Notes

  • Karthus has strong poke and consistent mid-late game damage; avoid extended trades.
  • Engage before he can safely use Lay Waste and Defile for max damage.
  • Be aware of his ultimate; ward and track enemy jungler to avoid surprise kills.
  • Manipulate waves to bait him into overextending for trades or all-ins.
  • Post-level 6 all-ins are safer if his ultimate is on cooldown.
