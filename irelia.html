Matchup Notes

  • Dodge this matchup if possible. Irelia becomes extremely oppressive after building sustain items and lifesteal.
  • The combination of Merc Treads, Blade of the Ruined King, and Wit's End is very difficult to handle as Diana.
  • She excels at freezing waves with sustain, stacking her passive from minions, and punishing lack of AOE from Diana.
