Matchup Notes

  • Dodge this matchup if possible; it’s very difficult after she builds Null-Mantle and Lifesteal.
  • Merc Treads, Blade of the Ruined King, and Wit's End make her extremely hard to beat as Diana.
  • She excels at freezing waves due to sustain, passive stacking on minions, and limited AoE spells.
