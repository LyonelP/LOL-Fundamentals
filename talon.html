Matchup Notes

  • This matchup depends a lot on his rune choice. Best scenario he goes Phase Rush or Electrocute. If he goes Conqueror, you have to be extremely careful going for E trades especially once he has ult.
  • He'll normally start W level 1 and you can trade for Q. Getting hit by the first part of W doesn't matter but make sure to avoid the second part.
  • Diana R can be used to interrupt Talon’s E.
