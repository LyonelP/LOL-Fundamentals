Win Conditions

I think win conditions are greatly misunderstood in League of Legends. Win conditions are almost always about outperforming your enemy by a very small degree, which could be anything from:

  • Having itemized slightly better
  • Getting 2 more CS per minute
  • Executing any of the skills taught in this course

Understanding and consistently applying small advantages across the game is what creates a true win condition.
