50%

Matchup Notes

  • Riot buffed Veigar’s Q range to 1050, so he can out-range Diana’s 900 Q.
  • It’s usually best to move up to the wave after he uses Q to farm, minimizing his poke.
  • When trading with your ultimate, wait to Q until you are unable to auto-attack within his Event Horizon cage.
