Diana Mid Matchup – Akshan
Back to Matchup Hub
48%

Matchup Notes

  • Move up with the ranged creeps level one and stay center in the lane as Akshan can look for an E trade and autos level 1 with his passive.
  • Level 2 is just as difficult as he can combo E with Q and potentially the movement speed from q. Wait to move up until he uses Q so he can’t chase down and proc press the attack.
  • Don’t be afraid of giving up a lot early in this matchup because it completely switches after level 6.
  • Once you’re level 6 you can look for ult trades. This is one of the few matchups where you can hold R a second and still interrupt his E.
  • Akshan is a great target throughout the game. Don’t be afraid to look for solo ults to lock him down and take him out of the fight.
  • Don’t get caught off by invisible Akshan returning to the lane especially if you’re pushing the mid tower since he can use it to E off of it.
  • Continually check for MR buys from Akshan
