Diana Mid Matchup – Akshan
← Back to Matchup Hub
48%

Matchup Notes

  • Akshan has strong ranged poke; avoid extended trades early.
  • Be cautious of his passive resets in skirmishes.
  • Waves can be manipulated to zone him and punish overextensions.
  • Post-level 6, his all-ins become dangerous if you misposition.
  • Use vision and control mid brushes to reduce surprise engages.
  • Consider defensive early items if he snowballs.
