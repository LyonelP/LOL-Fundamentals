Understanding Matchups

Learning champion matchups is essential to make good decisions.

Key points:

  • Enemy Cooldowns: Know your opponent’s abilities and summoner spell cooldowns to know when you can safely engage.
  • Ability Interactions: Understand how your abilities interact with theirs.
  • Power Spikes: Identify levels or items where you are stronger than your opponent.
