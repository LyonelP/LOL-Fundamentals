Understanding Matchups

Learning champion matchups is essential to improving your lane performance and overall game understanding. Matchups dictate how you trade, when you can be aggressive, and how you position throughout the game.

Key points to consider in matchups:

  • Identify which champions you can trade favorably against.
  • Recognize champions that punish your champion’s cooldowns or skillshots.
  • Adapt your wave control and roaming opportunities based on your matchup.
  • Understand both the early and mid-game power spikes for your opponent.
