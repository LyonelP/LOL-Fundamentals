Generic Ping

Used to ping everything in the tab window like summoner spells, ults, and upcoming objectives. Also great for marking enemy objectives.

Danger

Warns teammates of enemy roams, ganks, possible engages, flanks, TPs, and incoming abilities.

Assist Me

I need help securing an objective or winning/surviving a fight.

On My Way

Only ping when you’re close to joining the fight.

Enemy Missing (MIA)

Alerts teammates to play safe because an enemy is no longer in vision. Ping once if the enemy is unlikely to commit to the roam and twice or more when it is likely.

Warded

Area needs vision for your team or the enemy team has warded that area.

Push

Push the lane to crash the wave. Mostly used after a gank and when low on resources.
