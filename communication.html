Team Communication

The best way to communicate with your team is through pings. Ping twice, check if it was received, then ping again if needed. Chat is best for morale. Time spent typing is time not playing.

Use “gj” after good plays, “ty” if a buff is handed over. Behind or enemy makes a good play? Use “np we’re fine” or “we’ll win”. Thumbs-up emote works too.

Generic Ping

Used to ping everything in the tab window: summoner spells, ultimates, and upcoming objectives. Great for marking enemy objectives too.

Danger

Warn teammates of enemy roams, ganks, engages, flanks, TPs, and incoming abilities.

Assist Me

I need help securing an objective or winning/surviving a fight.

On My Way

Only ping when you’re close to joining the fight.

Enemy Missing (MIA)

Alerts teammates to play safe because an enemy is no longer in vision. Ping once if unlikely to roam, twice or more if likely.

Warded

Area needs vision for your team or enemy has warded that area.

Push

Push the lane to crash the wave, mostly after a gank and when low on resources.
