Communication

The best way to communicate with your team is through pings. Pinging twice and checking if it was acknowledged works best. Chat is best for morale. Avoid typing excessively, as it takes focus away from the game.

Typing “gj” after a good play or “ty” when someone hands over a buff is great. When behind, “np we’re fine” or “we’ll win” reassures teammates. The thumbs-up emote works well as a fast alternative.

Common Pings

⚠️

Danger
Warn teammates of enemy ganks or roams. Use sparingly.

🛡️

Assist Me
I need help securing an objective or fight. Ping before engaging.

➡️

On My Way
I'm coming to help. Only ping when close to the objective.

❓

Enemy Missing
Enemy laner disappeared into fog of war. Helps teammates play safe.

👁️

Warded
Area is warded by your team or enemies. Plan vision control accordingly.
