Communication

The best way to communicate with your team is through pings. Pinging twice and checking if your communication was received before pinging again works best. Chat is best used for keeping your team morale high. Time spent typing is time not spent playing, so minimize it.

Typing “gj” after a good play is great, and “ty” if someone hands over a buff. When behind or the enemy makes a good play, typing “np we’re fine” or “we’ll win” works wonders. Using the thumbs-up emote instead of typing “gj” is also faster and effective.

Generic Ping: Used to ping everything in the tab window like summoner spells, ults, and upcoming objectives. Also great for marking enemy objectives.

Danger: Warns teammates of enemy roams, ganks, possible engages, flanks, TPs, and incoming abilities.

Assist Me: I need help securing an objective or winning/surviving a fight.

On My Way: Only ping when you’re close to joining the fight.

Enemy Missing (MIA): Alerts teammates to play safe because an enemy is no longer in vision. Ping once if unlikely to commit to roam and twice or more if likely.

Warded: Area needs vision for your team or the enemy team has warded that area.

Push: Push the lane to crash the wave. Mostly used after a gank and when low on resources.
