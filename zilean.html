Diana Mid Matchup – Zilean
← Back to Matchup Hub
52%

Matchup Notes

  • If you get hit by a Q, you can move into the wave, specifically the ranged creeps to force a push from him or to make them hard to last hit under tower when you crash the wave.
  • Look to push him in early since his Q will ruin his ability to last hit under tower.
  • After 6 it's all about baiting his ult out. This can be done by forcing an all in and leaving him with low health. Then he'll have to watch out for every potential combo.
