Matchup Notes

  • Ahri’s best trade lvl 1 is to auto W auto towards the river bush (blue side) or top river side (red side) moving towards Diana with the movement speed from W. She often dodges Q by moving inside the Q arc. Counter this by throwing only max range Q’s and waiting for her to start autoing the wave.
  • It's crucial to thin out the wave against Ahri to prevent her from trading effectively with autos and landing easy charms.
  • Build Verdant Barrier or Banshee's Veil early in the matchup. Ahri doesn’t have high sustain damage, and the spell shield allows you to bypass Ahri’s E.
