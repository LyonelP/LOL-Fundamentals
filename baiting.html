Different Ways to Bait Your Opponent

Baiting is about manipulating your opponent into making mistakes or overextending.

Here are effective ways to bait:

  • Overextending in lane: Appear vulnerable to tempt your opponent into chasing you, then turn the fight with your jungler or teammates.
  • Taking a slightly less favorable trade: This can include taking a tower shot to make the enemy think they have an advantage, drawing them out of safety.
  • Positioning opposite your jungler: Stand on the opposite side of the lane from your jungler to encourage the enemy to commit to a fight, making it easier for your team to collapse on them.
