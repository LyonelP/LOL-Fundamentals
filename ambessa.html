Matchup Notes

  • Ambessa is difficult to trade with early due to strong poke.
  • Watch for her level 6 power spike; all-ins become dangerous.
  • Maintain vision in mid lane to avoid surprise rotations or ganks.
  • Wave management is key; keep her pressured to limit roaming.
  • Consider defensive items or summoner spells if she starts snowballing.
