Diana Mid Matchup – Lux
← Back to Matchup Hub
48%

Matchup Notes

  • Lux has long-range poke; avoid getting hit by Q-E combos early.
  • Use your E to dodge skillshots and engage after her cooldowns.
  • Wave management is key—freeze near your tower when she has summoner advantage.
  • All-ins post-level 6 are stronger if you bait her ultimate first.
  • Keep track of her shield and mana usage to exploit short trades.
