Diana Mid Matchup – K'Sante
← Back to Matchup Hub
48%

Matchup Notes

  • K'Sante has strong early trades and can block your all-ins with his abilities.
  • Watch for his Facebreaker to avoid being pulled in and comboed.
  • Manipulate minion waves to force him out of position before committing.
  • All-ins post-level 6 are tricky; time your abilities around his Ult for optimal trades.
  • Keep a safe distance during extended trades; his crowd control can turn fights quickly.
