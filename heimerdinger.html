Matchup Notes

  • Let Heimer push in early. He's an easy target for ganks and eventually runs out of mana, giving you an opportunity to kill him.
  • Heimer's turrets can be used to close the gap, setting yourself up for easy chases and good ult usage.
  • Later in the game, his main way of killing you is with an ult turret into stun, so be aware of positioning.
