Matchup Notes

  • Bait out Anivia’s Q level 1 by walking up as if to auto the wave, then sidestep toward the direction she casts from for an easy dodge.
  • After baiting her Q, thin out the wave so she can’t safely auto you. Her low base health makes minion aggro punishing.
  • Be careful committing to her egg early; even if you have sustain damage, nearby enemies may rotate before you secure the kill.
  • Best approach is to poke her with Q to get her to lethal but avoid proccing egg. She’ll struggle to clear waves, letting your jungler finish her off later.
