Diana Mid Matchup – Anivia

47%

Matchup Notes

  • Bait out Anivias Q’s Level 1 by moving up the minion wave as if you were going for an auto but then move towards the side that Anvia is throwing it from for an easy dodge.
  • After baiting out the q think out the wave so can’t look for auto attack.She has a low base health so the minion aggro she takes from autoing will hurt.
  • Even with your sustain damage from passive, be careful committing to killing her in egg form. Especially early enemies will have time to rotate and you might not have lethal damage.
  • The best way to play the matchup is often to get her into lethal range with Q poke but to not proc the passive. She won't be able to push out the wave that way and you can wait for jungle assistance to finish her off.
