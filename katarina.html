Matchup Notes

  • Auto two different melee minions once after the first wave settles to disrupt Katarina’s ability to Q all three melees.
  • Crash the second wave. Katarina will hit level 3 first, which is her main early kill window—be mindful of jungle pressure.
  • Track incoming minion waves, as Katarina can Shunpo to minions to disengage or dodge your E.
  • If you win a trade, hold E to match Katarina’s Shunpo and continue the chase.
  • Diana R can interrupt Katarina R, but most of her damage comes from passive resets, not Death Lotus.
  • Look for fast E trades when Katarina steps too far up—she needs setup and struggles with quick burst trades.
