Diana Mid Matchup – Katarina
← Back to Matchup Hub
49%

Matchup Notes

  • Auto two separate melee creeps 1 time after the first wave settles. This will mess up her ability to Q the first three melee minions.
  • Crash the second wave. She'll hit lvl 3 first which is her only chance to win the matchup. Be careful of ganks here.
  • Watch out for incoming minion waves as Katarina can E to minions to cancel your engage.
  • If you're winning a trade, save your E to follow Katarina's E away.
  • Diana R can cancel Katarina R, though Death Lotus makes up a small portion of Katarina's overall damage.
