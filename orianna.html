Matchup Notes

  • Playing against Ori relies a lot on knowing when she can move her ball. You can always trade Q for W, but avoid her Q + W unless you can stack a full W on the wave.
  • To win this matchup you have to look to E when she Qs out since she won’t be able to recall the ball before you finish the combo. This combo can be done without landing Q first — E → W → Q → auto. You can also dodge her Q + ult this way if you think she might look for it.
  • Look to draw Ori out of lane by moving into fog of war.
  • This matchup is quite unwinnable now. Her strong early game plus her ability to go seraphs and exhaust prevents you from having enough damage to kill herlater on.
