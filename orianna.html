Diana Mid Matchup – Orianna
← Back to Matchup Hub
50%

Matchup Notes

  • Playing against Ori relies a lot on knowing when she can move her ball. You can always trade q for w but avoid her q w unless you can stack a full w on the wave.
  • To win this matchup you have to look to E when she Qs out since she wont be able to recall the ball with her before you finish the combo. This combo can be done without landing a q first so just e w q auto. You can also dodge her q ult this way if you think she might look for it.
