Matchup Notes

  • Ahri has strong poke and mobility, be cautious when engaging early.
  • Look to trade after she uses Spirit Rush or Charm to avoid counterplays.
  • Waves can be manipulated to bait her into overextending.
  • All-ins are strong post-level 6 with proper combo timing.
  • Track her energy bar; low energy can be an opportunity to engage safely.
  • Use minion waves to avoid landing charm directly on you.
  • Consider Roaming after pushing lane to punish her if she overextends.
