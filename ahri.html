Diana Mid Matchup – Ahri

Matchup Notes

  • Ahri’s best trade lvl 1 is to auto w auto towards but river bush on blue side and top river side on red side moving towards Diana with the movement speed from w and often dodging the q by moving inside the q arc. Counter this by only throwing max range Q’s and waiting for her to start autoing the wave.
  • It's crucial to thin out the wave against Ahri to prevent her from trading well with autos and finding easy charms on you.
  • Build Verdant Barrier/Banshees Veil earlier in the matchup. Ahri doesn’t have high sustain damage and the spell shield will allow you to bypass the Ahri E.
