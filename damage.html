Damage Fundamentals

Maximizing your damage output is a core skill in League.

Key Concepts

  • DPS (Damage Per Second): Understanding how much damage your champion will deal with each ability against different enemies.
  • Ability Combos: Learn the optimal order and timing of your abilities to maximize damage.
  • Micro Mechanics: Proper mouse movement, animation cancelling, and ability timings can drastically increase your damage.
  • Target Priority: Identify which enemies to focus first. Prioritizing targets who are isolated or are squishy will allow you to deal more damage.

Tips

  • Practice combos in training mode to internalize timing and rotations.
  • Watch your champion's auto attack and ability animations to improve DPS in trades.
  • Track your and enemy cooldowns to know when it’s best to commit cooldowns.
