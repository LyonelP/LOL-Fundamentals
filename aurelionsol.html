Diana Mid Matchup – Aurelion Sol

53%

Matchup Notes

  • Aurelion Sol wins this matchup level 1 since they changed his Q. His best play is to look for 1 Q, proc comet, wait for the comet CD and go for a big trade by your range creeps. Don't get baited by that trade and play safe until level 3.
  • Since Aurelion Sol doesn't move during his Q channel, it's easy to land your Q on him. Go for most trades after landing free Qs. Your ult can also be used to interrupt his W.
  • Your window to win this matchup is between levels 3–7. After that he'll have a Giant's Belt and will start outscaling, especially once he has his upgraded E. The buffed ult with the slow alone can be used to pick off Diana.
