Diana Mid Matchup – Aurelion Sol
← Back to Matchup Hub
53%

Matchup Notes

  • Aurelion Sol wins this matchup level 1 since they changed his q. His best play is to look for 1 q, proc comet, wait for the comet cd and go for big trade by your range creeps. Don't get baited by that trade and play safe until level 3.
  • Since Aurelion Sol doesn't move during his q channel its easy to land your q on him. Go for most trades after landing free qs. Your ult can also be used to interrupt his W.
  • Your window to win this matchup is between level 3-7. After that he'll have a giants belt and will start outscalling especially once he has his upgraded E. The buffed ult with the slow alone can be used to pick off Diana.
