Diana Mid Matchup – Aurelion Sol
← Back to Matchup Hub
53%

Matchup Notes

  • Aurelion Sol has strong waveclear and roaming potential; avoid extended trades.
  • Look for engages when his stars are on cooldown for safer trades.
  • Post-level 6, dodge his roaming Ult to prevent giving up kills elsewhere.
  • Control vision in side lanes to track his movements effectively.
  • Use Diana's burst to punish his squishier early game moments.
