Matchup Notes

  • Lee Sin is very early-game oriented and looks to snowball off skirmishes and tempo plays.
  • He can win most early 1v1s if he lands Q and manages energy properly.
  • Vision control is extremely important — denying his Q angles reduces most of his threat.

Level 1 Ward

Ward his raptors or opposite buff to track early pathing and avoid getting invaded or counterganked on early scuttle fights.

Where to Start

Start opposite side and play for information. Avoid blind river fights before seeing his position.
