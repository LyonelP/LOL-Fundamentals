Target Selection

Generally, the best targets are those who are behind in the game, as they will be lower level and have less gold, resulting in lower base stats and fewer items. Basically, focus on the champions who will take the most damage the quickest.

Immobile carries, such as Draven, are also great options. How the enemies are positioned always plays a huge role in target selection. Prioritize enemies who are isolated or poorly positioned.
