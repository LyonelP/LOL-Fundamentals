Cassiopeia Mid Matchup
Back to Matchup Hub
48%

Matchup Notes

  • Look for Qs level 1 but avoid pushing the wave too hard so she can’t force an extended level 2 all-in.
  • Cassiopeia’s mana pool was buffed, but her E still costs 40 mana and Q costs 50 mana.
  • Bait out Cassio’s Q when approaching the wave, then safely step up afterward.
  • Be very cautious with trades due to her sustained DPS and healing. E trades without a reset should only be taken when she’s extremely low mana.
  • The best trades are when Cassio plays aggressively under your tower, where she can’t fully leverage her sustained damage.
  • Cassio’s E draws minion aggro, so adjust wave positioning accordingly.
  • Watch out for Cassiopeia’s W later. the slow scales up to 80% and you won’t be able to E out of it.
