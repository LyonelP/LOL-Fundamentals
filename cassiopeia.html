Diana Mid Matchup – Cassiopeia
← Back to Matchup Hub
48%

Matchup Notes

  • Look for qs level 1 but be careful to not push the wave too hard so she can’t look for an extended all in level 2.
  • They recently buffed her mana pool but each E costs 40 mana and Q 50 mana.
  • Watch our for Cassiopeias’ W later on as you won’t be able to E out of it and the slow scales to 80%.
