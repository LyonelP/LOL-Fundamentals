Diana Mid Matchup – Kassadin
← Back to Matchup Hub
51%

Matchup Notes

  • Don't trade qs at the very start of the lane as he’ll gain the shield and draw minion aggro from having target you with q pulling the wave his way.
  • Instead wait to move up until you need to last hit. He’ll likely be standing right on the melee creeps and your Q will kill all three ranged creeps and hit him.
  • If the wave was pushed, it's very easy to crash the second wave and let it reset on the third wave. Ideally you have the wave coming your way on the second if he's pushing for some reason.
  • Post 6 you have to wait for him to r on you. You pretty much always take that opportunity to go for an ult trade.
  • You should also always have the push in this matchup and will be able to 1v1 for most of the game.
