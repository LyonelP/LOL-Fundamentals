Map Awareness

Map Awareness is your ability to check the minimap frequently and quickly while gathering all of the information presented. Strong map awareness allows you to anticipate plays, avoid ganks, and make better macro decisions.

Things to Look Out For

  • Where your teammates are in lane: Did they just back, or are they playing up at the enemy tower? If they just recalled, playing aggressive is riskier. If they’re pushing up at tower, try to match their push. If you can’t, ping them back since you won't be able to capitalize off of them getting ganked.
  • Flashes and abilities being used: Keep mental or written track of key cooldowns across the map.
  • Jungle camp respawn timers: Tracking these helps you predict jungle pathing and future ganks.
  • Teleport opportunities: Watch for wards to TP to — or enemy TPs being channeled.
  • What’s warded: Be aware of vision control and which areas are safe or unsafe to move through.
  • What the enemy does after pushing in a wave: Are they roaming, warding, or recalling? Each gives different map information.
  • When the first tower falls bot lane: This usually means the enemy bot lane will rotate mid or top after resetting. Ping and prepare accordingly.
