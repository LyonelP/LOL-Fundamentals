Diana Mid Matchup – Ekko
← Back to Matchup Hub
52%

Matchup Notes

  • They just buffed Hail of Blades again so watch out for Ekko’s E trade level 1 and 2.
  • Once Ekko Qs the wave, play far up to prevent and let his minions finish off yours.
  • Once Ekko has enough ap he can start clearing the ranged creeps as early as level 7. This will give him a huge prio advantage if you can’t contest him before the q or immediately after he casts it on the minion wave.
  • Wave management is key to prevent him from roaming effectively.
  • Past level 2, the only other way to lose this matchup is to get hit by a w so watch out for them when he goes off to the side. Use E to a creep to dodge the w if you have to.
