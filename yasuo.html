Matchup Notes

  • This is Diana's main counter. His Wind Wall blocks both your Q and W orbs, which prevents the ability from going on cooldown.
  • Yasuo naturally counters Diana as an AD bruiser and can itemize early magic resistance while out-DPSing you.
  • Diana is one of the easiest champions to engage on, making her a reliable Yasuo ultimate target.
  • Always pull the wave early against Yasuo to avoid extended trades, set up ganks and to counter his ability to E forward.
  • The best trade window is when Yasuo E’s forward.
  • Use Q to break his passive shield and Bone Plating before committing with E. Your moonlight will outlast his shield.
