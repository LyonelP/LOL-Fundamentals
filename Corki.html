Diana Mid Matchup – Corki
← Back to Matchup Hub
52%

Matchup Notes

  • Corki has strong poke and burst; respect his Gatling Gun and Missile Barrage combos.
  • All-ins post-level 6 are easier to win if you dodge the Big One missile.
  • Manage wave control to reduce his long-range harassment.
  • Use brush and fog of war to surprise him and force unfavorable trades.
  • Coordinate with jungler for opportunities to punish Corki’s low mobility outside Valkyrie.
